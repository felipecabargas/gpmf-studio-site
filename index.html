What it does

GPS map preview

See your GoPro's GPS track on an interactive map before you export anything.

Motion sensor charts

Plot accelerometer, gyroscope, and gravity streams to see exactly what your camera recorded.

Export to GPX, KML, CSV, or JSON

Take your GPS track into mapping tools, or your raw sensor data into a spreadsheet or script.

Batch processing

Point it at a folder and export every clip inside, all in one pass.

Automatic chapter merging

GoPro splits long recordings into chapter files — GPMF Studio detects and merges them for you.

Light & dark mode

Follows your system appearance automatically.

FAQ

Is GPMF Studio open source?
No. It's free to download and use, but the source code is proprietary and not publicly available. See the license for details.
Does it collect any of my data?
No. GPMF Studio runs entirely on your computer and never sends your videos, GPS data, or anything else over the network. There's no analytics or telemetry in the app.
Why does my computer warn me when I open it?
The installers aren't code-signed or notarized, so macOS and Windows show a generic warning for unrecognized developers. See the instructions under each download button above.
Which GoPro models are supported?
Any GoPro that records GPMF telemetry into its video files — HERO5 and newer, including 360-degree models.
What can I export?
GPS tracks as GPX or KML, and any sensor stream (GPS, accelerometer, gyroscope, gravity, and more) as CSV or JSON.
